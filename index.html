Welcome

I'm Thomas Roussel-Roozmon, an International Grandmaster from Montreal, Canada. I earned my GM title in 2010 at the Khanty-Mansiysk Olympiad, becoming the fourth Canadian-born player to achieve this distinction. I've had the honour of representing Canada at three Chess Olympiads (Torino 2006, Dresden 2008, and Khanty-Mansiysk 2010).

With over 20 years of teaching experience, I've helped numerous students progress from 1500-1600 to 2200-2300 ratings. I'm a positional player with a strong focus on strategic understanding, deeply influenced by studying the games of Karpov and Kramnik. My specialization lies in endgame mastery—both playing and teaching this crucial phase of the game.

Beyond chess, I hold degrees in Philosophy, Mathematics, and Economics, and have also worked as a professional horticulturist after completing studies at the Montreal Botanical Garden. I believe this diverse background enriches my teaching approach and communication style.
